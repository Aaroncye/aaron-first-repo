D+ APP ICON.

The inspiration for this app icon was from Disney+, as you can see from the colors and design!

Design Rationale
  1. The issues I see in this app icon design, is that there is a lack of eye-catching elements.

  2. I hope to accomplish this by making the “D” in Disney emphasized with the plus on its own.

  3. This provides a clean and simple look that not only is aesthetically eye pleasing, but standouts out to the original smaller design of the Disney plus logo.

  4. I have chosen to stick with the overall color theme as Disney plus visual identity is minimalist and strict, and its dark blue colour palette adds a sense of luxury and chic to the whole image, elevating the look of the simple-built emblem and its three elements.
